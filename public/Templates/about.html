Team Project Hunt
Project Hunt is a platform that connects employers and potential employees through projects.

Hello there! And welcome to our app.
We made Project Hunt as Junior Developers in Elevation Academy's Code Bootcamp. The inspiration came to us after seeing that Elevation Academy was partnering up with employers such as Bizzabo, IBM and Jolt, and we thought it would be really cool if we could translate this scenario into a fully functional web app that can make a lot of Junior Developers and Employers really happy. We hope you enjoy it, and remember we are always looking for feedback!
